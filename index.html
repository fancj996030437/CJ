How to Play

EchoSqueak 2D is a time manipulation game where you guide a laboratory rat through dangerous puzzles. The unique twist: you never go back in time, but instead create echoes of your past actions to progress forward!

Controls:

  • Arrow Keys - Move
  • Spacebar - Jump
  • R - Restart
  • Shift - Create Echo

About EchoSqueak 2D

Created by developer guselect in just 3 days for "Code For A Cause 2025", EchoSqueak 2D features a unique gameplay mechanic where you create echoes of your past actions to solve puzzles. The game combines adorable pixel art with clever level design to create a challenging yet satisfying experience.

The procedural tail animation of the rat character adds a charming detail to the game's already polished visuals. With relaxing music by abstraction, the game provides an immersive puzzle-solving atmosphere that keeps players engaged level after level.

Pro Tips:

Plan your moves carefully before creating echoes. Sometimes you'll need to think several steps ahead to position your echoes in the right places. Don't be afraid to restart a level if your echoes aren't optimally placed!
